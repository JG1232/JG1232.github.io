About AMC

Learn about the American Mathematics Competitions and our mission

What is AMC?

The American Mathematics Competitions (AMC) are a series of examinations and curriculum materials that build problem-solving skills and mathematical knowledge in middle and high school students.

Founded by the Mathematical Association of America (MAA), the AMC program leads to the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO), the world championship mathematics competition for high school students.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to increasing interest in mathematics and developing problem-solving abilities through friendly competition at the middle school and high school levels.

Our goal is to inspire students to pursue advanced mathematics and related fields, fostering a love for mathematical thinking and discovery.

Competition Levels

AMC 8

For students in grade 8 and below, covering middle school mathematics topics.

AMC 10

For students in grade 10 and below, featuring more advanced high school mathematics.

AMC 12

For students in grade 12 and below, covering pre-calculus mathematics.

Why Participate?

  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Build confidence in mathematical abilities
  • Connect with like-minded students
  • Prepare for advanced mathematics competitions
  • Enhance college applications
  • Discover the beauty and power of mathematics
